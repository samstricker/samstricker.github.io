I'm Sam and you've reached my personal website have a look around. I'm a PhD student in Chemical Engineering at Imperial College London. My current research at Imperial College London focuses on leveraging artificial intelligence and digital twin technologies to enhance manufacturing and laboratory operations. This work bridges chemical engineering with advanced computational methods to create more efficient, predictive, and sustainable processes.

I investigate the integration of AI and digital twins to transform how we design, optimize, and control complex chemical and pharmaceutical processes, making them more efficient, sustainable, and adaptable.
